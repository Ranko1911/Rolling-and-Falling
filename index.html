What is Rolling and Falling?

Rolling and Falling is a personal project that I have been working on for a while now. It is a collection of lore, homebrew and original content for a campaign that I am currently running. I have decided to share it with the world in the hopes that it may inspire others to create their own campaigns or to use some of the content that I have created in their own games.

What can you find here?

Here you will find information on items, places, NPCs and events that are part of the campaign that I am running. You will also find some homebrew content that I have created for the campaign. I hope that you find the content here interesting and that it inspires you to create your own campaigns.
